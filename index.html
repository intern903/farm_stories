From the field journal

The orchard was here
long before us.

Most farmland projects begin with empty earth and a promise. Mango Meadows began with three hundred grown trees, already fruiting, already shading the paths between them. We didn't plant a story — we found one, and drew twenty-six quiet boundaries around it.

You'll hear the difference the moment you arrive. Mature orchards hum: birds in the canopy, wind in real leaves, the smell of ripening fruit in April. That is what your family inherits on day one — not a rendering of what might someday grow.

We visited on a Sunday morning expecting a sales pitch. Instead we stood under a forty-year-old mango tree while our daughter chased dragonflies. We registered plot 09 the next week.

About us

We believe
in slow, soulful,
sustainable living.

The Farm Stories was born from a simple observation: the families who most need a connection to nature are the ones with the least access to it. Professionals. Parents. People building lives in cities who are slowly losing something they can't name.

Founded in Coimbatore, The Farm Stories identifies and transforms the most soulful land across South India into boutique orchard communities — places where weekends become sacred and where memories outlast generations.

Mango Meadows in Agali is our first promise made real. More are coming.

01

Curated land

Every TFS project starts with the land. We look for natural character — mature trees, clean air, elevation, water proximity. The land must already have a soul before we build on it.

02

Boutique scale

We cap every community at 30–40 families. Small enough to know your neighbours. Large enough to share a pavilion, a trail, a harvest evening together.

03

Documentation trust

Clear title, clean patta, proper EC, registered layout. We make legal clarity a brand promise — not an afterthought. Every plot is investment-grade from day one.

Mango Meadows is a thoughtfully planned orchard community spread across 7 acres in Agali, at the foothills of the Nilgiris. Designed for families who value nature, privacy, and open spaces.

The community features 26 exclusive 20–30 cent plots surrounded by 300+ mature mango trees, breezy mountains and valley, natural greenery, and quiet landscapes.

Only 75–90 minutes — 45 kms from Coimbatore. Close enough to feel spontaneous. Far enough to feel free.

  • Secured perimeter fencing
  • Natural entrance arch with aesthetic design
  • 22-ft internal roads connecting the community
  • Landscaped greenery with 300+ trees retained
  • Street lighting for visibility and safety
  • Amphitheatre pavilion & community spaces
  • Walking trails & sunset decks
  • Ready-to-register with full documentation
